The possibilites are endless

The eruption of Mt. St. Helens was the most deadly and economically destructive volcanic event in the U.S when it blew back in 1980. Come up and take a look for yourself the true beauty of a volcano that forever engraved itself into the U.S history books.

Activities Galore

Nature brings many great activities to mind. Take a hike, go kyaking, catch some fish,
or simply watch the sunset with your signifigant other. With this many opportunities, it is
simply up to your imagination and how far it can take you! Where will your next adventure be?
Make sure to stop at all local visitor centers along the way to ensure you are getting the
maximum potential out of your stay up at Mt. St. Helens!
